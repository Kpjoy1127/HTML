FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.

This legendary match also brought renown for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a world record, which stand to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominny, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
